Product Marketing & Innovation

My core strength is the ability to bridge marketing strategy with product development, leading the Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy for transformative digital services that enhance user experience and drive institutional value.

FastLane OIP (Offer in Principle)

FastLane OIP is IDP's innovative digital service, matching users' qualification input with universities' eligibility requirements. It enables students to receive an "offer in principle" from partner universities within minutes. FastLane delivers a faster, more transparent, and confidence-building experience for students while helping institutions connect with qualified applicants more efficiently.

As Marketing Director, I led product marketing and positioning on Hotcourses websites. Defined the narrative for this innovative service that provides students with instant, confidence-building eligibility checks.

Strategic outcomes: Accelerated the application process, enhancing student experience and increasing the volume of qualified applicants for partner universities.

FastLane Propose

FastLane Propose is another IDP's innovative digital offering, leveraging global audience, student demographic and subject data demand, and AI to help institutions deliver personalised proposals to influence high-quality students to apply using their unique values such as scholarships, accommodation discounts, admission fee wavers, etc.

As Marketing Director, I provided critical product feedback and strategic guidance on expanding the top-of-funnel student pool. Designed data-rich traffic acquisition and retargeting campaigns for high-quality audience segmentation.

Strategic outcomes: Supported the delivery of personalised, AI-driven proposals to influence high-quality student applications, leveraging global demographic and subject demand data.

Student Community

Student Community is a peer-to-peer engagement platform embedded across IDP's digital ecosystem, enabling prospective students to connect with current student ambassadors through live chat, content feeds, and FAQs. It helps students have real insights into life and learning abroad while offering institutions scalable visibility and content.

As Marketing Director, I contributed to client pitch decks, developed the comprehensive SEO-driven content strategy, and led the GTM execution. Collaborated with Product/Development on ideation, design, and integration.

Strategic outcomes: Provided prospective students with authentic insights while offering institutions valuable visibility and content.
